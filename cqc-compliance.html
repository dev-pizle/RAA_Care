Our Commitment to CQC Compliance

RAA Home Support & Recruitment Ltd is committed to delivering safe, effective, compassionate, and high-quality care.

We operate in line with the standards set by the Care Quality Commission (CQC).

The CQC's Five Key Questions

Our services are structured around these standards.

🛡️

Is the service Safe?

We ensure robust safeguarding procedures and safer recruitment practices to protect service users and staff.

✓

Is the service Effective?

Care is evidence-based, person-centred, and regularly reviewed to achieve positive outcomes.

❤️

Is the service Caring?

Our staff are trained to deliver care with compassion, dignity, and respect.

⚡

Is the service Responsive?

We listen to feedback, adapt to individual needs, and respond promptly to enquiries and concerns.

📋

Is the service Well-led?

Clear governance, quality monitoring, and continuous improvement underpin everything we do.

How We Meet CQC Standards

  • Robust safeguarding procedures
  • Safer recruitment practices
  • Ongoing staff training and supervision
  • Clear governance and quality monitoring systems
  • Continuous service improvement

Registration Status

RAA Home Support & Recruitment Ltd is currently preparing for CQC registration and will commence regulated activities upon approval.
