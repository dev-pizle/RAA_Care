About Us

RAA Home Support & Recruitment Ltd was established with a clear vision: to provide reliable, compassionate, and professional home support services that promote dignity, independence, and wellbeing.

We understand that receiving care at home is deeply personal. Our approach is person-centred, respectful, and tailored to individual needs.

Our leadership is committed to strong governance, regulatory compliance, and continuous improvement to ensure safe and high-quality care delivery.

Our Registered Manager will be responsible for the day-to-day management of care services and ensuring compliance with CQC regulations.

Aims and Objectives

RAA Home Support & Recruitment Ltd aims to provide high-quality, safe, compassionate, and person-centred domiciliary care services that promote independence, dignity, and wellbeing for individuals living in their own homes.

The service is committed to delivering care that is tailored to individual's assessed needs, preferences, and cultural background. We promote choice, independence, and dignity always and ensure that service users feel safe, respected, and listened to.

We aim to recruit, train, and retain competent, caring, and professional staff who can deliver care in line with best practice. The service works in full compliance with CQC regulations and relevant legislation and seeks to work collaboratively with families, professionals, and external agencies to achieve positive outcomes.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I arrange care?
Get in touch by phone or our contact form. We'll have a conversation about your needs and, if you wish, arrange a visit to discuss a care plan and costs. There's no obligation.
Are your carers trained and checked?
Yes. We recruit, train, and retain competent staff using safer recruitment practices. We are a registered provider and work in full compliance with CQC regulations.
Can I have the same carer each time?
We aim to provide consistent carers wherever possible so you can build a relationship and feel comfortable. We'll discuss this when we plan your care.
What areas do you cover?
Please contact us to confirm whether we cover your area. We'll let you know and, if we do, we can arrange a visit or call to discuss your needs.
