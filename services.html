Our Services

Regulated domiciliary care in your own home. We tailor our support to your assessed needs, preferences, and cultural background.

Domiciliary Care Services

We provide personalised care and support to individuals in their own homes. All care plans are individually tailored and regularly reviewed.

Personal Care

  • Assistance with washing and bathing
  • Dressing and grooming
  • Toileting and continence care

Medication Support

  • Prompting and assistance with prescribed medication

Companionship

  • Social interaction
  • Support with hobbies and activities

Meal Preparation

  • Nutritional support
  • Special dietary requirements

Domestic Support

  • Light housekeeping
  • Laundry assistance

Emotional Support & Companionship

We offer emotional support and companionship so you feel listened to and supported. We can also help you access community activities and attend appointments.

  • Emotional support and companionship
  • Assistance to access community activities
  • Support to attend appointments

Our carers are trained to promote choice, independence, and dignity. We work with you so you feel safe, respected, and listened to.

Recruitment Services

RAA Home Support & Recruitment Ltd also provides recruitment support to healthcare providers. We supply trained care staff to support care delivery where appropriate.

We ensure:

  • Safer recruitment practices
  • DBS checks
  • Right to work verification
  • Reference checks
  • Compliance with safeguarding standards

Our recruitment service does not replace regulated care responsibilities; it supports care delivery with competent, caring, and professional staff who are trained in line with best practice.

How We Work

From first contact to ongoing care, we keep things clear and person-centred.

1

Enquiry

You get in touch by phone or our contact form. We listen to your situation and answer your questions.

2

Assessment

We arrange a visit (at home or by video) to understand your needs and preferences in detail.

3

Care plan

We create a personalised care plan with you and agree on visits, tasks, and costs.

4

Ongoing care

Care starts with a carer we match to you. We review regularly and keep in touch.
